Castles
=======
	- Zerg like aliens (spred bubbley organic creep over the map, but has no walls)
	- Machines / robots (Steampunk / clockwork style, lots of cogs, bronze, iron, steam and stone)
	- Good Mortals (Humans, elves, dwarves)
	- Evil Mortals (Undead, demons, underground)

Elemental affinities
====================
	- Good Mortals:	Physical > Water > Lightning
	- Evil Mortals:	Physical > Blood > Fire
	- Machines:		Fire > Earth > Lightning
	- Aliens: 		Poison > Cold > Lightning
